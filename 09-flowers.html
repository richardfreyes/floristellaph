Know your Flowers

Get to know the meaning behind your flowers.

Red roses convey deep emotions

Be it love, longing or desire. Red Roses can be used to convey respect, admiration or devotion.

White Roses

White is the color of purity, chastity and innocence. White flowers are genrally associated with new beginnings and make an ideal accompaniment to a first-timer bride walking down the aisle. White flowers can be be used to convey sympathy or humility, They are also indicative of spirituality.

Yellow Roses

Yellow roses are an expression of exuberance. Yellow reoses evoke sunny feelings of joy, warmth and welcome. They are symbols of friendship and caring.

Pink Roses

There are a lot of variations of the pink rose. OVer all, pink roses are used to convey gentle emotions such as admiration, joy and gratitude. Light pink rose blooms are indicative of sweetness and innocense. Deep pink rose blooms convey deep gratitude and appreciation. Pink rose also connote elegance and grace.

Orange Roses

The fiery blooms signify passion and enerfy, Orange roses can be used to express intense desire, pride and fervor. They also convey a sense of fascination. These flowers rival only the red roses as messengers of passion in romance.

Mixed Roses

By mixing rose blooms of different purposefully, you can create a bouquet of emotions. For example, a bouquet of red and white roses would mean I love you intensely and my intentions are honorable. A random mix of roses would convey mixed feelings or send a message: "I dont know what my feelins are yet but I sure do like you enough to send you roses."
